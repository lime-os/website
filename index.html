What's included?

Window Manager

A custom X11 tiling/floating hybrid, it's fast, elegant, and easy to configure.

Display Manager

A custom lightweight and elegant display manager that matches the design of our window manager.

Debian Distribution

A stripped down version of the Debian Linux Distribution, ensuring stability and security.

Popular Extensions

Enhance the functionality of LimeOS beyond the bare minimum with extensions.

Network Manager

Easily manage your network connections through an intuitive graphical interface, accessible directly from the top bar.

Audio Manager

Control your system's audio levels and devices with a simple mixer panel, conveniently integrated into the top bar.

System Requirements

LimeOS is designed for smooth performance on both low-end and high-end hardware.

Minimum Requirements

  • Processor: 1 GHz, single-core
  • Memory: 1 Gigabyte
  • Storage Space: 3 Gigabytes

Recommended Requirements

  • Processor: 1.5 GHz, dual-core
  • Memory: 2 Gigabytes
  • Storage Space: 6 Gigabytes
